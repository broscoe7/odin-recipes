Cucumber Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • sliced cucumber
  • 2 slices of bread
  • mayonnaise
  • ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each of the bread slices.
  2. Sprinkle black pepper generously over the mayonnaise on each bread slice.
  3. Put the cucumber slices evenly on the mayonnaise side of one of the pieces of bread.
  4. Put the other piece of bread on top, with the mayonnaise facing the cucumber, and press down lightly.
  5. Enjoy!
