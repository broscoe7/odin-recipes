Greek Salad

Description:

If eating vegetables seems like a chore, try making this simple salad and covering the vegetable flavour with a dressing. It's healthy and surprisingly filling.

Ingredients:

Salad

  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, large-diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, large-diced
  • 1 pint cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 1/2 pound feta cheese, crumbled

Dressing

  • 6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 clove of garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix the salad ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the dressing ingredients and pour over the salad.
  3. Enjoy!
