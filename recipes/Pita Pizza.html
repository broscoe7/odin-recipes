Pita Pizza

Description:

It's Friday night and you're tired and you want to sit back and watch a movie with some comfort food, but you don't want to go out and get anything. What should you do? Make a pita pizza!

Ingredients:

  • pita bread
  • pizza sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • toppings

Steps:

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the pita bread.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Add pizza toppings as desired.
  5. Put the pita pizza in the oven until the cheese melts.
  6. Take the pita pizza out of the oven, and enjoy!
